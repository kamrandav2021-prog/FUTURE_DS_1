1. Which products generate the most revenue?
Answer: Printers and Bookcases are the top profit drivers overall (as seen in the bottom-right sub-category chart). However, Clothing continues to heavily dominate transaction volume, accounting for 63% of all total quantities sold.

2. How do sales change over time?
Answer: Sales show significant seasonality throughout the year. The business starts strong in Q1 (January–March), but experiences a major slump and negative profits during the summer months (May and June). There is a massive recovery and peak performance in November (reaching nearly 10K in profit), likely due to holiday shopping or festive sales, followed by another dip in December.

3. Which categories or regions are most profitable?
Answer: * Category: Clothing is the clear leader in volume at 63%, followed by Electronics at 21% and Furniture at 17%.

Region/State: Maharashtra is the most profitable state, closely followed by Madhya Pradesh. Uttar Pradesh and Delhi bring in significantly lower profits compared to the top two.

4. Where should the business focus to grow faster?
Answer: * Capitalize on November Festive Peak: Plan heavy marketing campaigns, optimize supply chains, and ensure high stock levels well ahead of November to maximize the biggest sales surge of the year.

Fix the Summer Slump (May–June): Investigate why profits drop below zero in May and June. Introduce targeted mid-year clearance sales, summer promotions, or discounts to prevent losses.

Expand in Top States: Since Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh drive the vast majority of profits, concentrate growth strategies and targeted regional ads there while working to revive the lower-performing markets like Delhi.

Encourage Digital Payments: COD (Cash on Delivery) accounts for a massive 44% of orders. Offering minor incentives for UPI (currently at 21%) or Debit Cards could help reduce return-to-origin (RTO) risks and improve cash flow.
